ABOUT RRTS

The Road Repair and Tracking System (RRTS) is a comprehensive platform aimed at optimizing road maintenance for our cityâ€™s Public Works Department. It addresses resident complaints by automating prioritization of road repairs based on real-time assessments of road condition, area type, and traffic impact. Through RRTS, repairs are effectively managed, ensuring seamless coordination of resources.

RRTS empowers supervisors by enabling a systematic approach to complaint analysis and resource estimation. Scheduling repairs becomes efficient and dynamic, with real-time resource updates to accommodate sudden changes or emergencies. This adaptability allows for a more responsive and organized maintenance workflow.

With detailed reporting features, RRTS also provides city officials with real-time insights into the progress of road projects. These reports cover completed, ongoing, and pending repairs, offering transparency in infrastructure management. Our ultimate goal is to support city infrastructure and maintain safe, quality roads that benefit the community.

Type of road repair problems

Pothole Formation

Potholes form due to the freeze-thaw cycle, causing pavement cracks and collapses that lead to hazardous conditions on the road.

Crack Sealing

Sealing cracks prevents water penetration and erosion, extending the lifespan of roads by reducing structural damage from weather.

Surface Erosion

Erosion from rainfall and vehicle wear can degrade road surfaces, requiring periodic maintenance to prevent further deterioration.

Uneven Pavement

Uneven surfaces lead to hazardous driving, necessitating resurfacing or leveling to restore safety and functionality to the road.

Drainage Issues

Inadequate drainage causes water accumulation, leading to structural damage and requiring enhancements to maintain road quality.

Road Reconstruction

Road reconstruction involves extensive resources and often causes disruptions, making it a costly and time-consuming endeavor.

Road Repair Services

Pothole Repair

We ensure rapid and robust filling of potholes for safer roads. Durable materials are used to prevent future damage and enhance driving conditions.

Crack Repair

Our service seals cracks with weather-resistant materials to maintain smooth roads and reduce repair costs by preventing further deterioration.

Flooding Repair

We clear drains, enhance drainage, and add permeable materials to reduce water damage and ensure roads remain navigable during heavy rains.

Erosion Repair

Our service reinforces road edges, preventing further erosion and ensuring the stability of infrastructure in erosion-prone areas.

Sidewalk Repair

We repair damaged sidewalks to ensure safe pedestrian paths, addressing trip hazards and filling cracks to meet safety standards.

Road Resurfacing

Our road resurfacing enhances road durability by applying new asphalt, smoothing out bumps and potholes for a safer driving experience.
